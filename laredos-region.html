Laredos Region Socioeconomic Handbook

The Bi-National Socioeconomic Handbook (BSH) of the "Laredos Region" incorporates demographics, social, and economic data of the cities of Nuevo Laredo, TAM in Mexico and Laredo, TX in the U.S., with the purpose of illustrating the development potential of the region at a national and international level.

BSH publications contain yearly updated information and statistics, produced and validated by public departments and institutions as well as private sector organizations from both Laredo and Nuevo Laredo.

These handbooks are presented in both English and Spanish and serve as a reference tool for businessmen, investors, entrepreneurs, scholars, researchers, students, and anyone interested in learning more about the region.
